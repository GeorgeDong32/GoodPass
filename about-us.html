About us

We are a group of enthusiastic developers

Open to everyone

We strive to make security simple and accessible, making our security tools accessible to everyone.

We welcome people with lofty ideals to join us and contribute to our open source projects to build open security tools for the benefit of all.

Providing Reliable Software

We ensure that our security tools remain relatively secure through transparent development processes and timely updates. We also have a well-established testing process to ensure that every release can properly protect your secrets. Our open-source security tools also welcome scrutiny from all parties to help us better improve and combat vulnerabilities.
